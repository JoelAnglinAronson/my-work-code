2023 Dodge Charger Performance Specs

The 2023 Dodge Charger is precision-engineered for unrivaled track and street performance. Four muscular engine options deliver power that ranges from spirited to hellacious. An eight-speed automatic transmission and rear-wheel drive (RWD) are standard, while V6 models are available with all-wheel drive (AWD).

Performance Pages, included on most trims, provide access to track timers, a G-force monitor, a dynamometer, boost pressure, and dynamics optimizations. A high-performance or adaptive suspension, and four- or six-piston Brembo® calipers are available to provide exceptional control through every apex.

For those who don a race suit on the weekends, the Charger offers innovative track-inspired technologies for enhanced performance on select trims. A line-lock system lets you warm up your tires at the line; an SRT Power Chiller® helps cool the supercharger heat exchangers during every pass; and after every run, the race cooldown function reduces the temperature of the supercharger air cooler after the engine is shut off.

2023 Dodge Charger Interior & Technology

The driver-centric interior of the 2023 Dodge Charger makes your most-used and performance-critical functions easily accessible whether you’re on the highway or in the staging lanes. Deluxe amenities, such as available heated and ventilated front seats and heated rear seats, provide serene comfort for everyone and 16.5 cubic feet of trunk space ensures ample room for your gear. Available interior features include:

  • Five-passenger seating
  • Cloth, Nappa leather/Alcantara suede, or Laguna leather upholstery
  • Heated, ventilated, power front seats
  • Heated rear outboard seats
  • Heated flat-bottom steering wheel with mounted paddle shifters
  • Dual-zone automatic temperature control with rear air vents
  • LED interior lights
  • Auto-dimming rear view mirror
